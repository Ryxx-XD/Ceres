THE VISION

CERES is an autonomous, AI-driven ecological monitoring and navigation vessel engineered to revolutionize precision aquaculture. Designed specifically to support sustainable fish farming operations, CERES bridges the gap between complex hardware robotics and predictive ecological data science.

By autonomously traversing water bodies, mapping physical boundaries, and analyzing synergistic water toxins in real time, CERES safeguards aquatic ecosystems, optimizes feeding environments, and proactively prevents catastrophic mortality events—starting with the vital Oreochromis niloticus (Nile Tilapia) fisheries.

CORE ENGINEERING PILLARS
Autonomous Navigation & Kinematics
Merging GPS geospatial streaming, IMU sensor fusion, and mmWave radar obstacle avoidance into a dual-propeller differential thrust system controlled by closed-loop PID guidance.
Edge Intelligence & Spatial Mapping
Implementing adaptive algorithmic pathmaking and division to ensure comprehensive, energy-efficient spatial sampling across complex pond geometries.
Predictive Bio-Analytics
Utilizing multi-tier regression models to calculate real-time habitability indices, translating critical environmental vectors—such as pH, temperature, and ammonia gas interactions—into actionable, long-term survival projections.
